td { font-family: Courier, Arial, sans-serif; font-size: 10pt; } table { empty-cells:show; } &#xA0; &#xA0; &#xA0; S.B.&#xA0;No.&#xA0;1833 &#xA0; &#xA0; &#xA0; &#xA0; AN ACT &#xA0; relating to the use of a social media platform in furtherance of an &#xA0; offense involving the delivery of a controlled substance; &#xA0; increasing criminal penalties. &#xA0; &#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS: &#xA0; &#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;SECTION&#xA0;1.&#xA0;&#xA0;Subchapter D, Chapter 481, Health and Safety &#xA0; Code, is amended by adding Section 481.142 to read as follows: &#xA0; &#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0; Sec. &#xA0; 481.142. &#xA0; &#xA0; USE OF SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM FOR DELIVERY OF &#xA0; CONTROLLED SUBSTANCE. (a) "Social media platform" has the meaning &#xA0; assigned by Section 120.001, Business &amp; Commerce Code. &#xA0; &#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0; (b) &#xA0; &#xA0; If it is shown on the trial of an offense under Section &#xA0; 481.112, 481.1121, 481.1123, 481.113, 481.114, 481.119, 481.120, &#xA0; or 481.122, involving the delivery of a controlled substance that &#xA0; the defendant used a social media platform in furtherance of the &#xA0; offense, the punishment for the offense is increased to the &#xA0; punishment prescribed by the next higher category of offense, &#xA0; except that the punishment for a felony of the first degree is &#xA0; increased by five years and the maximum fine for the offense is &#xA0; doubled. &#xA0; &#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;SECTION&#xA0;2.&#xA0;&#xA0;The change in law made by this Act applies only &#xA0; to an offense committed on or after the effective date of this Act. &#xA0; &#xA0; An offense committed before the effective date of this Act is &#xA0; governed by the law in effect at the time the offense was committed, &#xA0; and the former law is continued in effect for that purpose. &#xA0;For &#xA0; purposes of this section, an offense was committed before the &#xA0; effective date of this Act if any element of the offense occurred &#xA0; before that date. &#xA0; &#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;&#xA0;SECTION&#xA0;3.&#xA0;&#xA0;This Act takes effect September 1, 2025. &#xA0; &#xA0; &#xA0; &#xA0; &#xA0; &#xA0; &#xA0; ______________________________ ______________________________ &#xA0;
